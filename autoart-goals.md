# Autoart Goals — Still Life

## CRITICAL PRIORITY: Object Density First

The canvas has 717 marks but the CENTER is nearly empty (77 marks). The bottle and bowl — the two most important objects — aren't dense enough to read as shapes. **Fix this before anything else.**

### Iteration Strategy
1. **FIRST:** Pack the wine bottle silhouette (center-left, x=-80 to -20, y=-100 to 150) with 40+ tightly clustered marks. Dark glass colors (#1a0a2e, #2d1b3d, #3b1f4a, #0d0d1a). Size 2-5, high opacity. Make it READABLE as a bottle.
2. **SECOND:** Pack the fruit bowl (center-right, x=20 to 120, y=-20 to 100) with 40+ marks. Warm fruit colors — reds, greens, yellows, purple grapes. Dense clusters for individual fruits.
3. **THIRD:** Only after objects are recognizable, add highlights, shadows, cloth, atmosphere.

## Theme
A Dutch Golden Age still life: wine bottle, fruit bowl, draped cloth on a wooden table.

## Aesthetic Priorities
- **Pointillist density** — hundreds of small marks building solid forms, not scattered blobs
- **Warm light from upper left** — golden highlights, deep shadows lower-right
- **Recognizable objects** — the bottle, bowl, cloth, and table should be identifiable shapes
- **Layered depth** — background marks at low opacity, structure at medium, focal highlights bright
- **Rich warm palette** — ambers, deep browns, burgundy, gold, cream. Minimal cool colors.

## What NOT to Do
- **DO NOT add atmosphere, shadows, light rays, or cloth until the bottle and bowl are dense and recognizable**
- No big marks (size > 10). Everything should be textural.
- No random scatter. Every mark should be part of a recognizable form.
- No even distribution. Dense where objects are, sparse where space is.
- Don't work on edges/periphery — the CENTER needs all the attention right now.

## Zone Priorities (strict order)
1. **Wine bottle silhouette** (center-left) — MUST BE DENSE. 100+ marks minimum.
2. **Fruit bowl + fruits** (center-right) — MUST BE DENSE. 100+ marks minimum.
3. Table surface (bottom third) — only after 1 & 2 are solid
4. Cloth folds (front edge) — only after 1 & 2 are solid
5. Background/atmosphere — LAST priority
